User Guide
Advanced Search
Large Base
81,683 PubMed article abstracts are included for completed randomized controlled trials from 2000 to 2021 year
Question Query
Search by PMID, Keyword or Question: E.g. "Do chloroquine or hydroxychloroquine improve outcomes in COVID-19 infection?"
Logic Query
Search by logic combination of terms: E.g. "hydroxychloroquine" and "personal protective equipment" or "remdesivir"
Element Annotation
Three types of elements: Population, Intervention, and Outcome (PIO) are annotated for each article abstract
Evidence Map
Hierarchical dependency relationships among PIO elements are visualized and clustered by Arm1 or Arm2
Download
Parsed PubMed article abstracts including annotated PIO elements can be downloaded in JSON format
